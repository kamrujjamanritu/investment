How It Works

A quick step by step guide to using the platform for investors, entrepreneurs and affiliate

Investors Entrepreneurs Affiliate

01

Create Account

Complete your profile and tell us what interests you. It's free to join the 308,500 angel investors already investing through our network.

02

Browse and Search

Filter by location, industry and investment size to find the right deal for you. We have thousands of opportunities so you will always find great deal flow.

03

Connect with Entrepreneurs

When you like a pitch, you can message the entrepreneur to ask questions, arrange calls and fix meetings.

04

Manage your Portfolio

As discussions progress, set the status and keep track of your investment decisions through your private dashboard.

05

Invest

When you're satisfied with your due diligence, you can finalize terms and invest directly with the entrepreneur.

01

Create Account

Sign up now to connect with investors and get funded. We have 308,500 angel investors worldwide with more joining every day.

02

Create a Pitch

Add a pitch for your business using our tried-and-tested template. The on-screen instructions will guide you through the steps. We've also got some great resources to help if you get stuck.

03

Publish your Pitch

When your pitch has been approved, it will be listed on the site for prospective investors to browse and evaluate. You can have your pitch emailed to investors and even send personalized messages to individuals.

04

Connect with Investors

If an investor likes your pitch, you will receive an email asking to connect. You'll then gain access to their contact details to continue discussions, schedule calls, fix meetings and close investment!

05

Complete your Fundraise

You can accept funds from investors at any time - you do not have to reach any funding target. Investors transfer to you directly so there is no commission fee from us.
